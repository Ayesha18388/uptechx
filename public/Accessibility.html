Accessibility Commitment

We are dedicated to providing an inclusive learning experience for all users. Our platform follows accessibility guidelines to ensure ease of use for everyone.

  • Keyboard navigation support for seamless browsing.
  • Screen reader-friendly content with proper alt text for images.
  • Color contrast optimization for better visibility.
  • Resizable text and zoom compatibility for readability.
  • Subtitle and transcript support for video content.
  • Accessible forms and navigation for assistive technologies.
